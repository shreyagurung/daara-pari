The Vision

My Idea of Home

"I didn't want to build a hotel. I wanted to build a place where people could just... stop. Where they could walk the village, eat food from our earth, and feel the kind of quiet that heals."

I envisioned Daara Pari not as a commercial venture, but as an extension of authentic village life. I try to curate every aspect of my property with a deep respect for the land and our people.

My philosophy is simple: keep it raw, keep it real, and let the mountains dictate the pace of the day.
