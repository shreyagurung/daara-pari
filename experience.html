Ways to Be

Tourism is observation. Here, I invite you to participate in our lives, or simply, to just be.

01 / The Essentials

Core Experiences

Village Walk & Exploration

Wander through our village with absolutely zero itinerary. Meet my neighbors (they love a good chat), listen to their stories, and experience the natural rhythm of the hills.

River Camping & Picnic

Spend a slow afternoon by the river. I'll pack you a picnic of fresh farm food—just promise me you won't check your emails while you're down there.

Sunrise & Sunset Drives

Chase the golden hours. I’ll drive you to Ramdhong to watch the world wake up, or we'll find a quiet spot to watch the sun slip behind the mountains.

02 / The Wild

Nature & Exploration

Forest Trails & Viewpoints

Follow the trails deep into the forest. Discover hidden viewpoints and the silent, untouched beauty of the wild.

Slow Scenic Drives

Take the long way around. Slow, intentional drives through the hills, where the journey itself is the destination.

03 / The Land

Cultural Immersion

Tea Plantation Visit

Walk through local tea estates and witness how the region’s famous leaves are nurtured, grown, and carefully harvested.

Seasonal Harvesting

Depending on the season, join me in the fields. We plant paddy in the monsoon, and harvest cardamom in the autumn. Honest, grounding work.

04 / The Mind

Slow Living

"Do Nothing" Time

The rarest luxury. Sit on the balcony. Watch the mist roll in. Let the hours pass without feeling the need to fill them.

Living Like a Local

Eat what I eat, live how I live. It's genuine, uncurated, and mostly consists of drinking way too much tea.

05 / The Spirit

Extended

Monastery Visit

A quiet trip to the local monastery. Sit with the monks, listen to the chants, and find a moment of absolute stillness.
