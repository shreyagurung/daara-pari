Welcome to Daarapari

I'm so glad you found your way here.

Here, every day unfolds a little differently. The light changes, the mist rolls in, something fresh is ready to be picked from the garden, and there's always a story quietly waiting to be lived.

This is the home I share with my family in Hee Bermiok, West Sikkim. A place that's been shaped by the seasons, the people around us, and the simple rhythm of everyday life.

Think of this as an invitation into my home.

As you wander through these pages, you'll find moments from the farm, the people who make this place what it is, the meals we share, the changing seasons, and the quiet rhythm of life.

And if, somewhere along the way, Daara Pari begins to feel a little familiar, I'd love to welcome you here for a while.

Welcome home.

-Sristi :)
