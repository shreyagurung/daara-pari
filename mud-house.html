A Mudhouse, and Beyond

A 1BHK mudhouse with a warm kitchen, a small herb garden, and a sunlit lawn. An outdoor shower open to the sky. Out back, a quiet stretch of green that opens into endless cardamom fields, where wild musk deer still wander.

A kitchen, in case you'd like to cook.

Step inside, and you're greeted by an open, sunlit kitchen. A skylight fills the space with natural light, and at its center sits a stone slab from our farm, used as both a prep space and dining table. It’s simple, intuitive, and made for slow, unhurried meals.

A spot to sink into.

A cozy mud sofa invites you to sink in with a book or your thoughts. A farm-sourced coffee table and a small work desk complete the space, holding both comfort and quiet productivity.

Where the day softens.

Beyond the living room, the bedroom opens into a quiet, earthen space. The bed, built from mud, holds you in a deep, grounding comfort, while a sunroof above frames drifting clouds, monsoon rain, or starlit skies, all from the warmth of your bed.

More than just a washroom.

Yes, even our bathroom deserves its own little paragraph. It’s a bit of a vibe. Morning rituals come with birdsong, fresh air, and the occasional feathery visitor. They seem to like it here too :)

This house works best if you don’t mind sharing a little space.
