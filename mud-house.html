The Mud House

A small house of earth and slate, on its own at the edge of the field.

Private 1BHK · Kitchen · Living space · Attached bath

₹7000 per night · Best for 1–2 people

One room, mostly window.

The walls are made by hand, so they aren't quite straight, and the light moves across them in a way that's hard to photograph. There is a bed, a stool, a blanket that smells of the line it was dried on. Nothing here is trying very hard.

A kitchen, in case you'd like to cook.

Two burners, a kettle, jars of things from the garden. We can leave you with rice, dal, eggs, whatever the season is offering. Or we'll send food across from the main house, whichever feels easier on the day.

What grows here ends up on the table.

It's a working farm, not a postcard of one. Some weeks there is more than we know what to do with. Some weeks the deer get there first. Either way, you're welcome to walk through the beds and pick what you want for the next meal.

And then, the doing of nothing.

There is a chair on the verandah that gets the morning sun. Most of our guests find it within the first hour and don't move very far for the rest of the stay. We think this is a perfectly reasonable use of a holiday.

This space works best if you’re not in a hurry.
