Every corner carries a story, shaped by bare hands.

The Space

A Mudhouse, and Beyond

A 1BHK mudhouse with a warm kitchen, a small herb garden, and a sunlit lawn. An outdoor shower open to the sky. Out back, a quiet stretch of green opens into endless cardamom fields, where wild musk deer still wander.

The Kitchen

A kitchen, that invites you to cook.

Step inside, and you're greeted by an open, sunlit kitchen. A skylight fills the space with natural light, and at its center sits a stone slab from our farm, used as both a prep space and dining table. It’s simple, intuitive, and made for slow, unhurried meals.

The Living Space

A spot to sink into.

A cozy mud sofa invites you to sink in with a book or your thoughts. A farm-sourced coffee table and a small work desk complete the space, holding both comfort and quiet productivity.

The Bedroom

Where the day softens.

Beyond the living room, the bedroom opens into a quiet, earthen space. The bed, built from mud, holds you in deep grounding comfort, while a sunroof above frames drifting clouds, monsoon rain, or starlit skies from the warmth of your bed.

The Washroom

More than just a washroom.

Yes, even our bathroom deserves its own little paragraph. It’s a bit of a vibe.

Morning rituals come with birdsong, fresh air, and the occasional feathery visitor. They seem to like it here too.

This house works best if you don’t mind sharing a little space.
